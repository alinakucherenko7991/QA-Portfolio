# Bug Report-003: Like counter does not update after tapping the Like button

**App:** Threads  
**Severity:** Low  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Environment:** iOS 18.6.2, iPhone XS  
**App Version:** Threads 327.0 (TestFlight)

---

## ✅ Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the Threads app  
2. Open any post  
3. Tap the Like button

---

## ✅ Expected Result:
The heart icon turns solid and the like counter increases by +1.

---

## ✅ Actual Result:
The heart icon changes state, but the like counter does not update.

---

## ✅ Additional Notes:
- After refreshing the feed, the counter updates correctly  
- No error messages displayed  
- Reproducible ~60% of the time
