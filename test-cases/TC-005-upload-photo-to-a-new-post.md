# TC-005: Upload Photo to a New Post

**Area:** Media / Posting  
**Priority:** High  
**Severity:** Major  
**Test Type:** Functional  
**Platform:** iOS / Android  
**Preconditions:**  
- User is logged in  
- App has permission to access photos  

---

## ✅ Steps:
1. Open the app  
2. Tap “Create Post”  
3. Tap “Add Photo”  
4. Select a photo from the gallery  
5. Add text  
6. Tap “Post”

---

## ✅ Expected Result:
Photo appears in the new post.  
Upload completes without errors.  
Post is visible in feed and in profile.
